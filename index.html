Store Migration

Moving to Shopify from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, or Squarespace? I handle the entire migration: products, customers, orders, SEO redirects, and theme. Zero data loss, zero downtime, zero headaches.

  • Full product catalog with variants, images, metafields, and SEO data
  • Customer accounts with order history and tags preserved
  • Comprehensive 301 redirect map for every indexed URL
